What're NFTs?
NFTs, or non-fungible tokens, are unique digital assets representing ownership of a specific piece of digital content. These can include artwork, music, virtual land, or collectibles and are often created using blockchain technology.
What is OKX NFT Marketplace?
OKX NFT Marketplace is a decentralized NFT trading platform where you can trade NFTs on over 10 networks. In addition to buying and selling on OKX NFT, people can also directly buy NFTs from other platforms including OpenSea, Magic Eden, and Blur. On our Launchpad, quality projects release on a regular basis. The secondary market supports rarity inquiries, order book, and bulk buys. Our Stats and Rankings sections provide real-time data on the blockchain to help with your decision-making.
How to create NFTs?
OKX NFT supports creating NFTs by uploading or AI creation. Select the network, add descriptions, and create your NFTs. After that, you can list NFTs for sale or keep it in wallets.
How to buy NFTs?
Connect your wallet, search or browse for NFTs on OKX NFT. Then buy directly, make offers, or bulk buy NFTs. Tap the cart icon to bulk buy NFTs.
How to sell NFTs?
Connect your wallet, visit My collected, and list NFTs for sale or transfer NFTs. Enter the price, currency, and expiry date or orders. Sellers can lower prices or cancel listings, but this requires network fees. You can easily check the order status in our OKX Wallet.
How to mint NFTs?
To join an initial mint of NFTs, create or connect a web3 wallet, have enough funds ready, and follow the instructions for mint. Keep your NFTs stored in wallets safely by securing your secret recovery phrase and private keys. Stay on alert against frauds.
How to invest in NFTs?
To invest in NFTs, try minting NFTs or trading NFTs in secondary markets. Set your budget and do diligent research. Pay attention to a project's history, community engagement, and assess its potential for value appreciation.
